Algorithm Description :
The Dynamic SIP Strategy adjusts the monthly investment amount based on market conditions 
rather than investing a fixed amount every month. 
The strategy analyzes 12-month momentum, moving average trend, and market volatility to classify 
the market as bullish, bearish, or neutral. During strong market conditions, 
the strategy increases investment contributions, while during weak or highly volatile conditions 
it reduces investment exposure. This adaptive approach attempts to improve long-term returns while 
reducing risk during unfavorable market periods.
